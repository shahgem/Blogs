
# Mastering Inventory Management with Advanced SQL Techniques in SAP Business One

This article explores how advanced SQL techniques can be used in SAP Business One to improve inventory visibility, classification, and decision-making through FSN and ABC analysis.

## Overview

Inventory management is one of the most important areas where data can directly improve business performance. By classifying inventory based on movement and value, businesses can identify slow-moving stock, high-value items, operational risks, and purchasing opportunities.

## Key Topics

- SAP Business One inventory data
- FSN analysis: fast-moving, slow-moving, and non-moving inventory
- ABC analysis for inventory value classification
- SQL-based inventory segmentation
- Inventory optimization and purchase planning

## Business Value

This analysis helps businesses improve stock control, reduce excess inventory, identify critical items, support purchase planning, and make more informed inventory decisions.
