# Understanding the Relationship Between Inventory Levels and Sales Using SQL Correlation Coefficient

This article explains how to analyze the relationship between inventory levels and sales using SQL and the Pearson correlation coefficient.

## Overview

Inventory and sales are closely connected. By measuring the correlation between inventory quantity and sales volume, businesses can better understand whether stock availability is supporting or limiting sales performance.

## Key Topics

- Inventory and sales relationship
- Pearson correlation coefficient
- SQL-based analysis
- Inventory optimization
- Data-driven decision-making

## Business Value

This analysis can help businesses identify whether inventory levels are aligned with demand, detect potential overstock or stockout issues, and improve purchasing and replenishment decisions.
