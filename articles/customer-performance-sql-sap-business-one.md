# Analyzing Customer Performance with Advanced SQL Queries in SAP Business One

This article explains how advanced SQL queries in SAP Business One can help analyze customer performance, sales behavior, and business partner activity.

## Overview

Customer performance analysis helps businesses understand which customers drive revenue, how customer behavior changes over time, and where opportunities or risks may exist. SAP Business One data can be used to build powerful SQL-based reporting for customer insight and decision support.

## Key Topics

- Customer and business partner analysis
- SAP Business One sales data
- Advanced SQL reporting
- Customer performance metrics
- Sales behavior and business insight

## Business Value

This analysis helps businesses identify valuable customers, monitor sales patterns, evaluate business partner performance, and support stronger sales and finance decisions.
